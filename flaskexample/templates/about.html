Crunchgrant


Your portal to research grants



Facilitating exchange of knownledge in the scientific community is the motivation behind Crunchgrant. If you want to find out which researchers have been awarded funding on your favorite topic, Crunchgrant is the tool for you!

Search features such as investigator name, institution, funding amount and any term in the abstract can be used as filters to extract the relevant grant proposals. In this first version, Crunchgrant has been built by aggregating research grants data from the National Science Foundation since 2000, and will be further expanded using other organisms in the U.S. and the world.

Try it now to connect with other scientists!
